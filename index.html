Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a Research Scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing, Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Sichuan University, China, in 2018, supervised by Prof. Dezhong Peng. From August 2016 to August 2018, I was a joint Ph.D. student at the University of Birmingham in the United Kingdom, supervised by Prof. Xin Yao.

My research interests mainly focus on machine learning and evolutionary many-objective optimisation. Related research topics include representation learning, multimodal learning, deep neural networks, subspace clustering, cross-modal retrieval, blind source separation, many-objective evolutionary algorithms, etc.
