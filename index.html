Welcome to my homepage. I am a PhD candidate at the Machine Intelligence Laboratory, College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Dezhong Peng. From 24 August 2016 to 24 August 2018, I was a joint PhD student at the Centre of Excellence for Research in Computational Intelligence and Applications (CERCIA), School of Computer Science, University of Birmingham, United Kingdom, under the supervision of Prof. Xin Yao, and financed by the China Scholarship Council.

My research interests are mainly in machine intelligence and evolutionary computation, particularly, machine learning and evolutionary many-objective optimisation. Related research topics include manifold learning, sparse coding, subspace representation, neural networks, blind source separation, pattern recognition, many-objective evolutionary algorithms, etc.
